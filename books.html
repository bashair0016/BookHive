Trending these days

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is a magical and inspiring tale about a young shepherd named Santiago who dreams of discovering a hidden treasure in Egypt. Along his journey, he learns valuable life lessons about listening to his heart, following his dreams, and understanding the universal signs around him. The novel beautifully blends adventure, spirituality, and self-discovery, encouraging readers to pursue their personal legends. It’s a timeless story about hope, destiny, and the transformative power of belief.

The Lord of the rings by J.R.R.Tolkien

The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien is an epic fantasy saga that follows the journey of Frodo Baggins, a hobbit tasked with destroying a powerful ring that could bring darkness to Middle-earth. Alongside a diverse fellowship, he faces incredible challenges, battles evil forces, and discovers courage and friendship. The story explores themes of heroism, sacrifice, and the struggle between good and evil. It is a richly imagined world filled with magic, adventure, and timeless lessons.

The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown

The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown is a thrilling mystery novel that follows symbologist Robert Langdon as he uncovers hidden secrets in famous artworks and historical landmarks. The story revolves around a conspiracy linked to the Holy Grail and secret societies, blending art, religion, and history. With puzzles, codes, and unexpected twists, it keeps readers on edge. The novel explores themes of faith, knowledge, and the clash between tradition and discovery.

The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, The Witch and the Wardrobe

The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe by C.S. Lewis is a magical story set in the land of Narnia, where four siblings—Peter, Susan, Edmund, and Lucy—discover a world trapped in eternal winter by the evil White Witch. With the help of Aslan, the noble lion, they fight to restore peace and justice. The novel explores themes of courage, friendship, and the battle between good and evil. It’s a timeless tale of adventure, imagination, and hope.

Harry Potter by J.K.Rowling

Harry Potter by J.K. Rowling is a captivating fantasy series that follows the life of a young wizard, Harry Potter, as he discovers his magical heritage and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Alongside his friends, he faces dark forces led by the evil Lord Voldemort. The story explores themes of friendship, bravery, love, and the fight between good and evil. It’s a magical journey filled with adventure, mystery, and unforgettable characters.

Great big beautiful life by Emily Henry

Great Big Beautiful Life by Emily Henry is a sweeping and emotional romance‑historical fiction novel. It centers on two writers — optimistic newcomer Alice Scott and cynical Pulitzer‑winning journalist Hayden Anderson — who both vie to write the biography of a mysterious, reclusive heiress Margaret Ives. As they dig into Margaret’s past, they uncover secrets, scandals, and conflicting memories, leading them to question whose story is the “true” one. Meanwhile, amidst rivalry and unraveling mysteries, Alice and Hayden’s relationship grows — blending romance, moral dilemmas, and family legacy into a rich story about identity, truth, and love.

Alice in Wonderland by Lewis Caroll

Alice in Wonderland by Lewis Carroll is a whimsical fantasy story about a young girl named Alice who falls down a rabbit hole into a magical, nonsensical world. She encounters curious characters like the Cheshire Cat, the Mad Hatter, and the Queen of Hearts, navigating strange adventures and puzzling situations. The story explores imagination, curiosity, and the absurdities of life. It remains a timeless tale celebrated for its creativity, humor, and playful logic.
